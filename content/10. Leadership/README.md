# Leadership

## Goal

Build interview-ready leadership depth for Senior, Staff, and Principal TPM roles by covering influence, ownership, decision making, executive presence, stakeholder alignment, and the behavioral competencies most evaluated at MAANG+ companies.

## Estimated Duration

## Outcome

After this module, you should be able to:
- Demonstrate senior leadership behaviors in STAR format with clarity and depth.
- Apply influence without authority frameworks across engineering, product, and executive audiences.
- Run high-quality meetings, difficult conversations, and executive communications.
- Prepare a complete behavioral story bank aligned to MAANG+ leadership principles.
