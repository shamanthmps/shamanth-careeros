# Product Thinking

## Goal

Build interview-ready product thinking depth for Senior, Staff, and Principal TPM roles by covering strategy, discovery, prioritization, metrics, experimentation, and TPM-PM collaboration.

## Estimated Duration

9-12 hours

## Outcome

After this module, you should be able to:
- Apply product thinking to make programs outcome-focused, not feature-focused.
- Use prioritization, OKRs, and roadmap strategy to drive cross-functional alignment.
- Design and interpret experiments and A/B tests with statistical rigor.
- Demonstrate a mature, differentiated TPM-PM partnership model in interviews.
