# Behavioral Interviews

## Goal

Prepare Senior, Staff, and Principal TPM candidates to excel in behavioral rounds at MAANG+ companies by mastering STAR structure, building a deep story bank, and calibrating responses to Amazon, Google, and Meta interview frameworks.

## Estimated Duration

12-16 hours

## Outcome

After this module, you should be able to:
- Deliver tight, high-signal STAR answers across all behavioral categories.
- Build and activate a story bank covering 10+ behavioral competencies.
- Calibrate your answers to Amazon LP scoring, Google's four attributes, and Meta's scale-and-impact framework.
- Walk into any MAANG+ behavioral round with stories ready, anti-patterns eliminated, and company-specific framing applied.
