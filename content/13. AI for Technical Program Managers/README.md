# AI for Technical Program Managers

## Goal
Build practical, interview-ready AI literacy for Senior, Staff, and Principal TPM roles so you can lead AI architecture, delivery, governance, and risk decisions across product and engineering teams.

## Estimated Duration
12-16 hours

## Outcome
After this module, you should be able to:
- Explain AI architecture trade-offs in senior interviews.
- Choose between prompting, RAG, and fine-tuning with clear reasoning.
- Define quality, safety, and security controls for production AI systems.
- Lead cross-functional AI programs with measurable governance and delivery plans.
