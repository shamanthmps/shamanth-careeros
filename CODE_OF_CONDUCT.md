# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in CareerOS welcoming, respectful, and productive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Give feedback that is specific and useful.
- Respect different career paths, backgrounds, and experience levels.
- Keep discussion focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith participation
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community.

To report a concern, contact the maintainers through the repository's private security or maintainer contact channels where available.
